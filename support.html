Welcome to the support page for TomorrowAI. Below you'll find information to help you get started and troubleshoot any issues.

What is Tomorrow AI?

Tomorrow AI is a smart scheduling assistant that helps groups coordinate events effortlessly. Using AI, it evaluates availability and preferences to finalize the best event time for everyone.

How It Works

  1. Users create a group and invite members.
  2. Members propose activities and set potential timeframes.
  3. Invitees vote on preferences (thumbs up/down).
  4. AI evaluates all inputs and schedules the event based on invitee availability.
  5. Event details are finalized and shared with the group.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an activity?
Go to your group, click the plus button and enter the details.

How do I invite users?
Use the invite feature (person button with a plus) within your group to send invitations.

How do I reset my password?
Tomorrow AI uses passwordless authentication. To log in, enter your email, and you'll receive a sign-in link.

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the app settings under “Account” → “Delete Account.” This action is irreversible.
